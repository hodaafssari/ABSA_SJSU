About ABSA

The Ahlul-Bayt Student Association at San Jose State University is dedicated to creating a welcoming environment for students interested in learning about and following the teachings of Ahlul-Bayt.

We organize regular events, discussions, and gatherings to promote understanding, foster community bonds, and provide spiritual guidance to our members.
